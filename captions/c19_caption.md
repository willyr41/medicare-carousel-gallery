## Carousel 19: Drug Price Negotiation Round 3

All 15 drug manufacturers selected for the third cycle of Medicare drug price negotiations have agreed to participate. This means the federal government will negotiate prices on 15 more high-cost prescription drugs, with the new lower prices expected to take effect in 2028 for Medicare patients.

This is the third round of negotiations under the Inflation Reduction Act. The first cycle covered 10 drugs (prices effective 2026), the second cycle added 15 drugs (prices effective 2027), and now the third cycle adds 15 more. Meanwhile, the $2,000 annual out-of-pocket cap on Part D prescription drug costs is already saving seniors thousands of dollars in 2026.
