## Carousel: $13 Billion Medicare Advantage Boost

CMS just finalized the 2027 Medicare Advantage payment rates, and the numbers are massive. After initially proposing a razor-thin 0.09% increase, the agency reversed course and locked in a 2.48% boost — translating to more than $13 billion in additional funding for MA plans next year.

This is a major win for the 35 million seniors enrolled in Medicare Advantage. Higher payments to insurers could mean richer benefits, lower premiums, and expanded coverage options heading into 2027. But there is a catch: CMS is also banning audio-only telehealth diagnoses from risk adjustment scores, which could cut into insurer revenue by limiting how they code patient conditions.
