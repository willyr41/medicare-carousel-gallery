## Carousel 20: CMS Fraud Crackdown & DMEPOS Moratorium

CMS has announced a nationwide 6-month moratorium on new DMEPOS (Durable Medical Equipment, Prosthetics, Orthotics, and Supplies) supplier enrollment as part of a massive crackdown on Medicare fraud.

The agency is now using artificial intelligence and machine learning to flag suspicious claims before they are paid. AI finds the patterns, then human experts verify the fraud. This is part of a broader effort to protect taxpayers and Medicare beneficiaries.

Billions of dollars are lost to Medicare fraud every single year. That fraud drives up premiums and costs for everyone else. CMS is fighting back with technology, enforcement, and new enrollment controls.
