BREAKING: A federal judge just ruled that Aetna, Elevance Health, and Humana must face DOJ kickback allegations in court.

The Department of Justice intervened in 2025, building on a 2021 whistleblower complaint that exposed a massive scheme:

→ Insurers allegedly paid brokers "hundreds of millions" in exchange for steering seniors away from competitor plans
→ Humana and Aetna allegedly arranged payments hinged on LIMITING enrollment of beneficiaries with disabilities
→ Major brokers GoHealth, SelectQuote, and eHealth will also face litigation

The government is using the False Claims Act to recover the money.

This is one of the biggest Medicare Advantage lawsuits in history — and it's just getting started.

As Medicare Advantage enrollment grows past 33 million seniors, marketing scrutiny has reached an all-time high.
