## Carousel: Elevance Faces MA Sanctions

CMS just extended the sanction deadline for Elevance Health, giving the insurer until May 30 to fix years of data submission violations — or face enrollment suspension across its Medicare Advantage plans.

The issue? CMS alleges Elevance has been noncompliant with risk adjustment data requirements for seven years, allegedly submitting corrections via encrypted USB drives instead of using the required electronic systems. Elevance calls it a "policy dispute," not a data issue. But with 1.9 million seniors enrolled in their MA plans, the stakes could not be higher. If CMS follows through, new enrollment would be frozen — potentially disrupting coverage for millions.
